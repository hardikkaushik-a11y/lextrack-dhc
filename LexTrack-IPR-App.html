Sources & legal notice

LexTrack reads publicly available court records. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by any court or government agency.

Data sources

  • delhihighcourt.nic.in — Delhi High Court cause-list and case-status pages
  • services.ecourts.gov.in — eCourts services portal

Legal basis — Court records are public under the Open Justice doctrine (Naresh Shridhar Mirajkar v. State of Maharashtra, SC 1966) and are not protected by copyright (Eastern Book Company v. D.B. Modak, SC 2008; Section 52(1)(q), Copyright Act 1957). Personal data within these records is processed under the Section 3(c)(ii) exemption of the DPDP Act, 2023, which excludes data made publicly available by a third party under a legal obligation.

Report a data issue or request takedown — legal@lextrack.app. We respond within 48 hours.
